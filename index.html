AcuaVerde Company Outing 2018

Creating a Batangas itinerary is a tricky exercise. The good news is, when it comes to beaches, Batangas has a lot to offer. The bad news: Batangas is vast and mountainous, and the best beaches are located so far apart from one another.

Last May 2018, JMaterials Corporation had their annual company outing at AcuaVerde Beach Resort and Hotel Inc. in Batangas. Divided into two teams, JMaterials people started their day with morning snack at the resort. After the snack, they proceeded with the morning activities such as Sack Race, Rope for Tug of War, Water Relay and Obstacle Race.

The games hosted by JC were quite exhausting. The teams rested for some time and then ate lunch together. After lunch, they decided to do some water sports activities like jet ski and banana boat. Some employees also did island hopping activity while others were just relaxing and drinking at the shore.

Christmas Party 2017

As with any other years, September 2017 was welcomed by Filipinos here and abroad by spreading virtual Christmas greetings and memes and blasting Jose Mari Chan’s iconic carols.

There’s now a certain magic in the air, and suddenly, everyone is in a festive mood and already making plans for noche buena, annual family and barkada reunions, and Christmas parties.

JMaterials Corporation held their christmas party last December 21, 2017 at 2 Central Function Room. The party was hosted by Ms. Gretchen and Mr. Rico. Aside from games, the party was held also to congratulate and award some of the best employees of JMaterials. The games had two parts. The first part was Flip Bottle Challenge and Tilt-A-Cup games. The second part was Bring Me Challenge, Spoon Feeding and Popping Balloon Challenge. There was also a raffle in the party. All employees and guests had a good evening with great food, lively music, dancing and drinking. Everyone enjoyed the party and was ended by a speech from the company owner himself.

Malaysia Tour 2017

Kuala Lumpur has grown into a modern and affluent metropolis from an erstwhile tin mining village. Despite its success, it remains a culturally diverse city. It is a melting pot of ethnic Malays, Indian migrants, British colonials and Chinese. Everywhere you go, you can see how the city managed to preserve its age-old heritage buildings, temples, and mosques that blend beautifully with towering buildings and opulent shopping malls.

Last July 2017, JMaterials people decided to have their annual company outing in Malaysia. We spent our three-day vacation exploring the Kuala Lumpur. It was such a good experience as not all employees have gone out of the country.

It was also a good time not just to relax but also to bond with co-workers. Jmaterials people were divided into groups for games and for the places we wanted to go. Some groups went to Kuala Lumpur Bird Park, some went to China Town. All employees went to Batu Caves. The trip was ended by giving awards to the deserving employees and of course picture taking at the restaurant.
