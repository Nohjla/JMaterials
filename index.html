About

We are mainly a marketing and sales support team. Our mission is to be a company with a reputation for eligibility, quality and integrity. We envision to be the leading company engaged in delivering quality service and outstanding value to both client and employee.

JMC Mission

"We provide reliable products with value for money to our clients. We deliver products and services of the highest possible value to our clients with reliability."

Company Policy

"Always be sincere to our clients and suppliers in order to build a long-term and reliable relationship."

Our Team

Ryosuke Nakagane

Mr. Nakagane is a director and a stock holder of Jmaterials Corp.He has business experience in Philippine for more than 10 years. Currently, he also serves as the President of Tokyo Grand Renovation (TGR) which isan affiliate company of Jmaterials Corp.

Naoki Tamura

Mr. Tamura has been in the sales industry for more than 15 years locally and internationally making him well-suited to be the General Manager of the company. He believes that in order to succeed a person should always be gentle and smart with his or her decisions. He also believes that smile can do magic. Mr. Tamura plays ice hockey and enjoys travelling. Both ice hockey and travelling are his stress reliever and relaxation.

Jeffrey John Zapata

Jay brings with him more than 20 years experience in the fields of selling and trading with clientele both local and international. His expertise and vast experience has helped position Jmaterials Corporation as one of the contenders in their field. He is also a good example to his colleagues and is seen as a mentor and an adviser. But more than his dedication and resilience at work, Jay is a family man and, he makes sure to spend quality time with his family as well as time for himself.

Shirley Inductivo Ramos

Shirley has more than 10 years of experience in providing professional accounting service to private businesses. She has been with Jmaterials since 2013 and oversees the day-to-day operations of company’s accounting department.She is responsible also in the preparation of all financial reports and preparation of tax returns and regulatory filings. She believes that the backbone of success is “hard work, determination, good planning, and perseverance. “ She also admires women who are mothers who balance family and work.
